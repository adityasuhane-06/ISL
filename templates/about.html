About Sign Sarthi

Bridging Communication Gaps Through AI-Powered Sign Language Recognition

Our Mission

Sign Sarthi is dedicated to breaking down communication barriers for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community. With over 70 million deaf individuals worldwide, we believe technology can bridge the gap between sign language users and the hearing world.

Our AI-powered platform recognizes Indian Sign Language (ISL) in real-time, translating gestures into text and speech, making communication accessible, instant, and inclusive.

What We Offer

Real-Time Recognition

Advanced AI models recognize Indian Sign Language gestures instantly with high accuracy.

Multi-Language Support

Translate ISL to multiple Indian languages including Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, and more.

Accessibility First

Designed with accessibility in mind, ensuring everyone can communicate effortlessly.

AI-Powered Learning

Our models continuously learn and improve to provide better recognition accuracy.

Cross-Platform

Access Sign Sarthi on any device - desktop, tablet, or mobile phone.

Community Driven

Built with input from the deaf community to meet real-world communication needs.
